AI Recruiter: No problem. A Dockerfile contains instructions such as `FROM` to specify the base image, `RUN` to execute commands (like installing software), `COPY` or `ADD` to bring in files, and `CMD` or `ENTRYPOINT` to define what runs when the container starts.
